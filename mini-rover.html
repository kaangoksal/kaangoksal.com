Mini rover v1.0

I've spent like 20 hours of SolidWorks time for this robot. I'm planning to use Arduino Nano V3.0, NRF24L01 (low range), L298N, 9V 350maH battery and 12V micro motors. 
Things I'm happy with: Nice design, Compact
Things I'm not happy with: Motors draw too much current, friction is too much = poor mechanics. Threads are useless it only looks cool.

Future plans: FPV!
