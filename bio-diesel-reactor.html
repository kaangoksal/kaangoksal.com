    In Turkey fuel is pretty expensive, and due that I’m living out of the city, I ride my motorbike a lot (it runs on fuel) The oil prices me and my family and also the country pays concerns me. While I was searching on the web, I have encountered various articles about biodiesel production using algea. The Idea of generating bio-diesel by only adding salts, CO2 and sun-light was inspiring and motivating.

   After I finish my applications and finals I’m going to contact local micro-biology labs to get algea and start growing them. I have a friend whoose dad owns a chemical factory, we are going to start the Project with him. His dad will help with the chemical reactions to seperate algea oil and convert it to diesel

  I’m planning to use Chlorella or Spirulina Algea’s for my Project.
