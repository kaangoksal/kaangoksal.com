Connecthing

Connecthing was an IoT startup founded by me and 3 other colleges. Our first product was a smart plug that connected to internet via Wi-fi. It had industry level energy sensors that showed the customers how much they consume in units of money. 
In Turkey we have dynamic energy pricing, the plug monitored the energy prices and suggested user to schedule energy consumption more efficiently.
